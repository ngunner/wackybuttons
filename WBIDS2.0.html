FAQ's

  • How come I cannot access certain features like saving and inserting pictures?

    You may have noticed that a message popped up on your screen asking if you trust the software and if you wish to allow it to run. In order to experience all the benefits of our online Design Studio you must allow or accept any messages that pop up. If you do not accept, then the tool may not start, or you will only have access to limited features.

    If you saw a message pop up and you rejected it, you can follow these steps to try again and accept the message:

    1. Close your web browser
    2. Open your web browser again
    3. re-visit this page (www.wackybuttons.com/studio.php)
    4. Accept or allow access for any messages that popup
    5. You should then have access to save your design and insert images from your computer.
