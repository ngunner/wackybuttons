Clothing Magnet

Clothing/Shirt Magnet Buttons

Our Clothing Magnet Buttons are a perfect alternative to our pin back buttons. The button has a flat back and the strong magnet separates from the button. Your clothing goes between the button and the magnet. They won't poke holes in clothes like buttons with pins. Great for hospitals and businesses.

Product Specifications

  • Warning: This product contains strong magnets which can be dangerous to children, people with pacemakers, electronics, and magnetic media. Full product warning can be found here.
  • The entire button is made of 5 pieces (Round Neodymium Magnet, Metal Back, Metal Shell, Paper Graphic, Clear Plastic Cover)
  • The magnet is strong enough to hold the button to a thick sweater or jacket
  • The metal components are made of tin-plated steel
  • We print using high quality Epson Photo Inkjet Printers (Digital CMYK).
  • The paper we use is a special ordered thin custom bright white paper with a matte finish specifically designed for superior color quality and brightness for inkjet printing.
  • All products are made in the USA
  • Please note: Our buttons are not waterproof. The metal will rust when exposed to moisture over time. The ink on the paper can also fade after being exposed to lots of sunlight over time.
